Deleting Your Account

CastWell - Deep Space Robots LLC

Instructions for deleting your account:

  1. 1. Log into the CastWell app
  2. 2. Navigate to the right-most tab (Settings)
  3. 3. Press the red "Delete My Data" button and confirm

All data associated with your account will be immediately deleted and unretrievable.
